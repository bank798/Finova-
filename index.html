FAQ

Clear answers before users commit.

Is CAPITALORA already a licensed bank?
This template does not claim that CAPITALORA is a licensed bank. Replace this answer with your verified legal structure, regulated partners, and the countries where services are actually available.
Are the balances and transactions real?
No. The balances, transactions, chart, and insights on this landing page are demonstration data used to preview the interface.
Will the website work on Android phones?
Yes. The page includes responsive breakpoints, a mobile navigation menu, touch-friendly controls, flexible card layouts, and support for narrow screens starting at 320 pixels.
Can I connect the buttons to my real application?
Yes. Replace the current login.html links with your real routes. The newsletter form is a front-end demonstration and should be connected to your backend or email provider before launch.
